Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta.
  • 2 chammach sarso oil.
  • 2 Lahasun ki kalee, kata hua.
  • one cup tomato, sauce.
  • one chopped onion.
  • 1 chammach salt.
  • 2 hari mirch and dhaniya patta katta hua.
  • 1 chammach haldi powder.

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until soft.
  2. ak pan mein sarason ka tel garm kare.
  3. lahasun, onion, hari mirch, dhaniya patta and haldi powder ko bhoon len.
  4. mix the pasta, sauce and salt. (jab tak swad ki mahak na aaye).
  5. than ready.

This recipe is a quick and easy dish, perfect for weeknights.
